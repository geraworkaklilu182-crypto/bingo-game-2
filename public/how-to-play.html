🎯 BINGO - HOW TO PLAY

🚀 QUICK START

1️⃣ Buy Cards - Click "+1 CARD" (costs coins from your wallet)

2️⃣ Start Game - Click "CALL NUMBER" or turn on "AUTO"

3️⃣ Mark Numbers - Click matching numbers on your cards

4️⃣ Get BINGO! - Complete 5 in a row → WIN COINS!

❌ WHEN YOU LOSE

😢 No BINGO - Game ends with all 75 numbers called, no pattern completed

💸 No Coins - Can't buy cards if wallet is empty

🃏 No Cards - Can't win without buying cards first

⏰ Time Out - If you don't mark numbers before next call (auto-mark helps!)

🎮 CONTROLS GUIDE

🎲 CALL NUMBER - Calls a random number (1-75)

🔄 AUTO - Automatically calls numbers every few seconds

🃏 +1 CARD - Buy one bingo card (costs game stake)

🃏🃏🃏 +3 CARDS - Buy three cards at once

🆕 RESET - Start a fresh game (clears all cards)

🔊 SOUND - Turn sound effects on/off

🗣️ VOICE - Turn number announcements on/off

🚪 LEAVE - Exit to dashboard

👑 ADMIN CONTROLS (Visible to Admins Only)

👥 Max Players - Set maximum players in game

⏱️ Call Interval - How fast AUTO mode calls numbers

💰 Game Cost - Cost to buy a card (10-100 coins)

🏆 Win Multiplier - Prize multiplier when you win

💸 Commission - Admin's cut from each win (default 20%)
